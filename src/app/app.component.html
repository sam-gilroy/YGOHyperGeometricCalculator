Term Definition
Starter A card which starts your decks main combo line
Extender A card which helps your deck continue playing after its combo is already started (a card CAN be both a starter and an extender)
Handtrap A card which is used from the hand to disrupt an opponents plays
Engine Requirement AKA "Brick" or "Garnet" this is a card which serves no direct purpose, but is required in order to make use of another card in the deck
Board Breaker A card which is meant to break up an opponents end board, usually when you are going second
Engine Any card which helps further your own game plan, also any in-archetype searchable disruptions
Non-Engine Any card which hinders your opponents game plan
